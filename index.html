About Dogs N Buns

Dogs N Buns is a fast-casual American-style hot dog restaurant located in Anjeong-ri, Pyeongtaek, South Korea. We specialize in classic hot dogs, chili dogs, combo meals, fries, and drinks, offering a simple, high-quality menu in a casual, quick-service environment.

Whether you're a U.S. military member looking for a taste of home or a local customer craving delicious American-style hot dogs, we're here to serve you fast, fresh, and flavorful meals.
